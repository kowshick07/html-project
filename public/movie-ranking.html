The Best Movies

My top 3 movies and series of all-time.

Game Of Thrones

Game of Thrones is a critically acclaimed HBO fantasy drama series that aired from 2011 to 2019, based on George R.R. Martin's A Song of Ice and Fire novels.

The Shawshank Redemption

The Shawshank Redemption is beloved for its powerful themes of hope, perseverance, and friendship, told through a deeply satisfying narrative featuring strong performances by Tim Robbins and Morgan Freeman, a brilliant script, and an incredibly rewarding emotional payoff that resonates with universal struggles against injustice and despair, even without relying on action or special effects.

F1

Sonny Hayes, a 1990s phenom who suffered a career-ending crash, returns to the sport decades later to save an underdog team, APXGP, alongside rookie Joshua Pearce..
